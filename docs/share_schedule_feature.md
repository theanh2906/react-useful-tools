# Share Schedule Feature Documentation

## Share Schedule Feature in Menstrual Tracking

The Share Schedule feature allows users to share their menstrual cycle schedule with others. This feature is designed to enhance the community experience and facilitate sharing of information among users.

### Usage Instructions
1. **Accessing the Share Schedule**  
   Navigate to the 'Menstrual Tracking' section of the application.
2. **Creating a Share Link**  
   - Click on the 'Share Schedule' button.
   - A modal will appear with options to customize the share link settings.
3. **Customizing Settings**  
   - Users can choose to share their schedule with specific users or create a public link.
   - Options to limit visibility date range can also be set within the modal.
4. **Sharing the Link**  
   - After customizing settings, click the 'Generate Link' button.  
   - The generated link can be copied and shared via messaging apps or social media.

### Notes on Public URL Handling
- Publicly shared links are accessible by anyone with the link.  
- Ensure appropriate privacy settings are established before sharing.
- Only users who have opted into public sharing will have their schedules visible through these links.

### Example Public URL  
```
https://example.com/share/schedule/{userId}
```
Replace `{userId}` with the actual user identifier to generate a valid link.

### FAQs
- **Can I revoke access to my shared link?**  
  Yes, users can revoke access at any time through the Menstrual Tracking settings.
- **Is there a limit to how many users I can share my schedule with?**  
  No, you can share your schedule with as many users as you wish.
